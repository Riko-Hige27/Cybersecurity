Rekik

PASSIONATE IT PROFESSIONAL & ASPIRING CYBERSECURITY EXPERT

I am an enthusiastic and self-motivated IT professional from the USA, with over 5 years of experience in technical support, network management, and data analysis. My career began as a Network Technician and evolved into roles in IT Helpdesk Support, where I developed a solid foundation in troubleshooting, user account management, and implementing security protocols. I am currently certified in CompTIA A+, CompTIA Server+, CompTIA Net+, CompTIA Security+, and the Google Cybersecurity Professional Certificate. My commitment to Cybersecurity drives me to excel in key areas such as incident response, network security, and vulnerability management. My programming experience with JavaScript, Java, React, Angular, and NodeJS, combined with my data analysis skills in Python, R, Tableau, and SQL, equips me with a comprehensive approach to identifying and mitigating security threats. I am dedicated to applying my diverse skill set to enhance Cybersecurity measures, safeguard organizational assets, and address contemporary Cybersecurity challenges effectively.
